How to change/unbind keys combinations in xfce-terminal.


Open Settings/Appearance, then goto tab “Settings” and check “Enable editable accelerators”.

Then in xfce4-terminal, click “Help” and hover over “Contents F1″. Press backspace or delete. For safety, disable the “Editable accelerators” again. This creates the file ~/.config/xfce4/terminal/accels.scm, which seems to contain all keyboard shortcuts.
